# TGSPDCL EV Charging Stations Consumption Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging Station consumption data under TGSPDCL (Telangana Southern Power Distribution Company Limited). The objective is to evaluate charging station performance, energy consumption trends, utilization rates, revenue generation, and demand growth using data analytics and visualization techniques.

The project was developed as part of a Data Analytics program and demonstrates practical skills in data analysis, KPI tracking, and dashboard development using Power BI.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze EV charging station energy consumption patterns.
- Evaluate station utilization and operational performance.
- Monitor revenue generation across charging stations.
- Identify demand growth trends and charging behavior.
- Develop an interactive dashboard for data-driven decision making.

---

## Dataset

**Source:**
https://data.telangana.gov.in/dataset/tgspdcl-ev-charging-stations-consumption-data

The dataset contains information related to EV charging station operations, including energy consumption, charging sessions, utilization metrics, and other performance indicators.

## Analysis Performed

### Station Utilization Analysis
Analyzed charging station usage patterns to identify highly utilized and underutilized stations.

### Energy Consumption Analysis
Examined energy consumption trends and charging demand across stations.

### Revenue Analysis
Evaluated revenue generation and operational performance of charging stations.

### Demand & Growth Analysis
Studied charging demand growth trends and future infrastructure requirements.

- Observed energy consumption patterns and demand fluctuations.
